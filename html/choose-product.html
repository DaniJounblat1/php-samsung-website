Commonly asked questions


Q: How do I select a product category?

A: To select a product category, follow these steps:

  • On the page, you will see different cards representing product categories.
  • Click on the card corresponding to the desired product category (e.g., Phones, Smart Watches, Buds, TV).
  • This will reveal a form where you can enter the serial number of your product.

Q: Where can I find the serial number of my product?

A: The serial number can usually be found on the product itself, not the box. Look for a label or engraved code on the device.

Q: What format should I use for the serial number?

A: The serial number should be entered as an 11-digit code. Make sure to input the digits correctly as specified.

Q: What happens after I submit the product form?

A: After submitting the form with the serial number, your request will be processed. If valid, you will receive further instructions to send your chosen product to our repair center.

Q: Can I submit multiple product repair requests at once?

A: Yes, the product form allows for the submission of multiple product repair requests at once. You can provide the details for each product in the form and submit them together.
